Frequently Asked Questions



Here you can find answers to the most frequently asked questions - if there is anything not answered here please feel free to contact us and let us know.


How do I find out about sporting events near me?

Using Trident you can either use your current location to find sports near your or search via an area using the search bar.


What types of sporting events do you offer?

We currently search for football, rugby, motorsport and ice hockey. If you would like to see a new sport added or add your team to one of the above please contact us and let us know.


How do I purchase tickets?

If tickets are available for a fixture you will be able to click the link to be taken to the authorised ticket distributor. Please note this will take you to an external site and is not associated with Trident.


What payment methods are accepted?

Payment types accepted will vary from vendor to vendor and each will be detailed on their site. Please note this will take you to an external site and is not associated with Trident.


What is your refund policy?

Refund policy will vary from vendor to vendor and each will be detailed on their site. Please note this will take you to an external site and is not associated with Trident.


How do I know if my tickets are legitimate?

Whilst every effort has been taken to ensure we link to official ticket sellers we urge you to ensure you trust the the site you purchase from.


Can I print my tickets?

This will vary from event to event. Please check before purchasing.


Can I transfer my tickets to someone else?

This will vary from event to event. Please check before purchasing.


How do I get notified about upcoming events?

You will soon be able to sign up for email alerts for your favourite team(s) or locations.


Can I create a saved list of events?

You will soon be able to crerate favourite team(s) or locations and get notified about new events.


Can I share events with my friends?

Absolutley - every event will have a share button so you can spread the word!


Can I buy tickets in bulk?

This will vary from event to event. Please check before purchasing.


Do you offer discounts for students, seniors, military or blue light members?

This will vary from event to event. Please check before purchasing.
